How to use the Quiz

First Step

Press the answer from the 3 options you have. You need to select just one option from those options displayed and forward the answer by pressing "Next Question"!

Second Step

You can go back and change your answer by pressing "previous Question" or just finish by pressing "Submit Quiz" and send the answers selected

Third Step

Final Step!

You can check the answers by going back, selecting different solutions and submitting them again to check if they are correct.
