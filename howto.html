How to use the Quiz

Step 1:

Read the question carefully and select the answer you believe is correct from the four options provided. Choose only one option and proceed to the next question automatically.

Step 2:

You can go back and change your answer by pressing "previous Question" or just finish by pressing "Submit Quiz" and send the answers selected

Step 3:

Once you have answered all the questions, you will receive a message indicating the number of answers you answered correctly. At this point, you have the option to reset the quiz by clicking on the "Reset" button if you wish to repeat the process.


By following these steps, you can effectively go through the quiz, receive feedback on your answers, and review your performance.
